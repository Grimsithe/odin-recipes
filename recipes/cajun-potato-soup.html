Cajun Potato Soup

Description

This is a delicious and hearty Cajun-style potato soup that's perfect for cold weather. It's packed with flavor and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp Cajun seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, melt butter and sauté onions and garlic until translucent.
  2. Add cubed potatoes and enough water to cover them.
  3. Cook until potatoes are tender (about 15-20 minutes).
  4. Add Cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour in heavy cream and stir until well combined.
  6. Simmer for 5 more minutes until soup is heated through.
