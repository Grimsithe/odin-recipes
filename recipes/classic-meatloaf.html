Classic Meatloaf

Description

This is a classic, comforting meatloaf recipe that's perfect for family dinners. It's moist, flavorful, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, onion, and garlic in a large bowl.
  2. Add salt and pepper and mix well.
  3. Pack the mixture into a loaf pan.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 45-60 minutes until internal temperature reaches 160°F.
