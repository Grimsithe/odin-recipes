Salsa

Description

This is a fresh and flavorful salsa recipe that's perfect for dipping or as a topping for tacos, burritos, or grilled meats. It's easy to make and packed with vibrant flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Dice the tomatoes and set aside.
  2. Finely chop the onion and garlic, and set aside.
  3. If using jalapeño, finely chop it and set aside.
  4. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  5. Mix well and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Let the salsa sit for at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to meld together.
